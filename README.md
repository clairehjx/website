# Claire's Website 💙

My personal website, where I share my coding journey and the apps I build.
Made with a "Dreamy Watercolor Blue" style because blue is my favourite colour
and I love watercolour art.

## What's inside

| File | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| `index.html` | The page itself (About me, My Journey, My Apps) |
| `styles.css` | All the colours, fonts, and the watercolour look |
| `script.js`  | Small touches: the year in the footer + fade-in-on-scroll |

## How to look at it on my computer

Just **double-click `index.html`** and it opens in a web browser. That's it —
there's nothing to install! 🎉

## How it's built (the computational-thinking bits)

- **Reusable design tokens:** all my colours are stored once at the top of
  `styles.css` (like `--blue`), so I can change the whole site by editing one line.
  That's *spotting a pattern* and not repeating myself.
- **IntersectionObserver:** instead of checking the scroll position over and over,
  the browser *tells my code* the moment a section appears. A smarter algorithm! 🧠
